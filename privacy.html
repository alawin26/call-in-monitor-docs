Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 19, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Call In Monitor ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Call In Monitor, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Email Address: To connect and access your Gmail account for monitoring purposes.
    • Client ID and Client Secret: Provided by you to authenticate with Gmail APIs.
    • Secret Key: A unique key provided by you to secure your account within our application.
  • Usage Data:
    • Employee Call-In Data: Information extracted from your Gmail account to monitor employee call-ins, including employee names, positions, and call-in times.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision:
    • To authenticate and connect your Gmail account using OAuth2.
    • To fetch and display employee call-in data from your Gmail account.
  • Security:
    • To ensure the security and integrity of your account and data within the application.
  • Improvement:
    • To enhance and improve the functionality of Call In Monitor based on user interactions and feedback.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our application, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your personal data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information held by us.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: You can disconnect your Gmail account from Call In Monitor at any time through the application interface.
